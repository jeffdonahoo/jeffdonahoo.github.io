Fellowships

There are many opportunities to receive monetary sponsorship for higher education, and, quite possibly, it is much easier than you think to successfully apply for such opportunities.  Fellowships are awarded to both students seeking both undergraduate and graduate degrees.  Baylor University actively encourages its students to seek national and international fellowships.  To learn more about the university's efforts at supporting fellowship applications, see their web page on National and International Scholarships.  Note that in some instances Baylor actually gives students money just for applying for fellowships, regardless of success.  Many universities have fellowships for their students (e.g. University of Texas) so if you have a program in mind at a particular university, inquire directly with the university concerning fellowship opportunities.  .

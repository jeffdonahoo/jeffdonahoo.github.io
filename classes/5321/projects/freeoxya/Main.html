In the Internet age, information wants to be free. Free from control, free from restraint, free for all who seek it. FreeOxy exists to liberate the World Wide Web from the chains of control, whether by governments, institutions, or Internet service providers. The goal of FreeOxy is to provide a means of serving www content to any who seek it.

FreeOxy is a peer-to-peer network of web proxies that routes URL requests Gnutella-style to find a node capable of serving the content. Better than manually searching out and configuring proxies one-at-a-time, FreeOxy leverages P2P networking to dynamically maintain a list of neighbor nodes for routing requests and serving proxy connections.

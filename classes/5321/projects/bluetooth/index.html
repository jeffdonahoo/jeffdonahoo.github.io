Overview

    Nuggets is a small project undertaken mainly to get a feel for the use of bluetooth for wireless communication. There were, of course, other reasons for writing Nuggets, such as grades, but we feel that those are secondary to the true educational value inherent in such a project.

     Nuggets is designed to be run on small wireless devices. The user stores a nugget of information about themselves on the device. When his device comes in range of other devices running nuggets, the devices will exchange nuggets. At the end of the day, the user can browse the nuggets of people he passed during the day.

     In addition to the obvious voyeuristic perks of such a service, we think most users will choose to put deep and meaningful information about themselves in their nuggets. This should result in a globally increased understanding of human nature and possibly bring about a golden age of compassion and empathy.

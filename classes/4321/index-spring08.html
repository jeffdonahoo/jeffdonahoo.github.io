        • Scale and Load Testing
          • Download Apache's JMeter and extract it to the directory. (<install> can be anywhere you wish.)
          • Copy protocoltests.jar into <install>/lib/ext.
          • Start JMeter by executing <install>/bin/jmeter.[bat|sh].
          • Load the test plan ossumatest.jmx into JMeter.
          • Click "Test Plan" and specify the server name/IP and port.
          • Execute the test by selecting Run->Start.
          • Click "Throughput Results Graph" to watch the show. Note the per minute throughput after it stabilizes.
          • Click "Client" under "Simulated Users" to change parameters. Note that donacipher.DataCipherSlow is an implementation of the DataCipher interface whose encryption is computationally expensive. You can use it from the JAR to play with your server.
